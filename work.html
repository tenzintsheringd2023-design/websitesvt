Projects

This is where I worked as the costume designer for the Bhutan baccalaureate play competition.

Drawbacks

  • Time management
  • Materials and fabrics
  • Cooperation
  • The structure of the whole costume plan

Solution

  • Made a group plan
  • Worked late when needed
  • Each person handled their own tasks
  • Materials and fabrics were provided by our teacher

Overall experience

Working as a costume designer was challenging. Initially, our cooperation and time management were poor, but we pulled everything together at the last minute. I was happy with the final result, making it a fun yet difficult experience.

Drawbacks

  • Lack of cooperation
  • Time management issues
  • Skill gaps

Solution

  • Encouraged by director's reaction, we worked harder
  • Practiced in extra personal time
  • Gave constructive feedback to each other

Overall experience

The play taught us to overcome challenges. Through feedback and teamwork, we improved skills, cooperation, and time management, ultimately achieving a performance we were proud of.

My Work

This page showcases my work, experience, and personal projects.

These are the overviews (pictures) of works I have done after coming to The Royal Academy.

Costume designer for the Bhutan baccalaureate play competition. In this project, I was responsible for creating designs that were both visually striking and practical for the performers. It required attention to detail, creativity, and collaboration with other team members to ensure every costume complemented the storytelling and stage environment.

Backstage team, costumes, makeup, stage, and narrator for the yearly schoolplay. Being part of the production allowed me to experience the coordination required behind the scenes. I learned the importance of teamwork, timing, and problem-solving under pressure while ensuring each performance ran smoothly.

Poster project with grade 8 on waste management awareness. This project gave me an opportunity to engage with the community creatively. I helped design educational posters that effectively communicated important environmental messages and encouraged sustainable practices among students and local residents.

Khorlo sculpture mold for aesthetic and technology project. This project challenged my design and technical skills as I worked on conceptualizing and executing a precise sculpture mold. I learned how to plan proportions carefully, work with materials, and bring a creative idea from paper to a tangible model.

Junior girls volleyball competition (placeholder image). Participating in sports events like this taught me teamwork, strategy, and leadership. Even outside the academic or creative projects, I learned how communication and trust between teammates play a crucial role in achieving collective success.
