1. Origami

One skills or strength i am proud of myself, is my interest in learning origami. The first environment thing I learnt to make was a crane from my friend. Before, I didn't even know how to make a simple aeroplane. But after I learnt how to make a crane form my friend I realized how fun and interesting it was. Moreover it also taught me how to be patient as I realized one single mistake in folding could ruin the whole output I wanted. Now I have learnt lots of things such as making flowers(which is really important if you want to gift someone a bouquet), butterlies, dragons, frogs, cats etc..and cranes(obviously). I mostly learn it from online tutorials.

About Me

Hi! My name is Tenzin Tshering Drukar. I am from Trashi Yangtse, the eastern part of Bhutan.

I am currently studying in The Royal Academy.

I used to study in Kheni Lower Secondary School, one of the most remote schools in Trashi Yangtse. Although it was very remote, I got my very first education from there, so I am truly grateful. Because of that school, I got the opportunity to study where I am today.
